# Rules of Tic-Tac-Toe:

- Players take turns placing their ring on the 3x3 board.
- The first player to get three of their rings in a row wins (They can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

<br>
<br>

# 過三關的玩法

- 玩家輪流放一個環在一個3×3的九宮格裏
- 第一個把三個環連成一條直線的玩家就能勝出 (可以用橫、直、斜的方式擺放環)

# rules of human tic-tac-toe

<br>

1. Split players into 4 teams 8 each.

<br>

2. Team members take turns to race to the destination with different movements.

<br>

3. Then, the player places a ring in a 3x3 square (just the same as tic-tac-toe)

<br>

4. Each team only has 3 rings.

---
class: bg-zinc-800 text-2xl
---

# rules of human tic-tac-toe

<br>

5. If all 3 rings are all placed in the 3x3 square, the player moves their rings to different boxes

<br>

6. Player can't move the opposing team's ring

<br>

7. As they race, they can't just run after the first round. Instead, they need to go in specific ways (Will be announced later 😉)

<br>

8. The player need to come back before next person starts

---
class: bg-zinc-800 text-xl
---

# 人肉過三關的玩法

<br>

- 將玩家分成 4 隊，每隊 8 人
- 隊員輪流用不同的動作到達終點
- 玩家將一個環放在 3x3 的九宮格裏（就像「過三關」一樣）
- 每隊只有3個環
- 如果3個環都放在了九宮格裏，玩家就可以將自己的環移動到其他的方格內
- 玩家不能移動其他隊伍的環
- 除了第一輪外，玩家不能只是跑，他們需要用特定的方式移動
- 玩家需要在前一個玩家回來後再出發
